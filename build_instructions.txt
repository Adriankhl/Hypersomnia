1. Download repositories: Augmentations and Hypersomnia and unpack them to some separate folders.
Links:
https://github.com/geneotech/Augmentations
https://github.com/geneotech/Hypersomnia
2. In your OS, set environmental variable AUGMENTATIONS to your Augmentations folder.
3. Open Hypersomnia.sln in Visual Studio and build everything.
